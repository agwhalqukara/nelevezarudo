We would love for you to join our team!  

Catawba Animal Clinic is looking for an outstanding Associate Veterinarian to join our well established but steadily growing hospital located in Rock Hill, South Carolina!  Our growth has been made possible by the confidence and loyalty of the three generations of clients that we have served since 1978.  

Every member of our team is compassionate and dedicated to delivering the highest level of care to every animal that comes through our doors. Our hospital is well equipped with advanced equipment to provide quality medical care to each of our patients. Including but not limited to:

  • *Fear Free* Certified Practice
  • 3:1 Staff to DVM ratio
  • (7) Well equipped exam rooms
  • Dedicated Dental Suite
  • Digital Dental Radiography
  • Laser therapy
  • Ultrasound
  • In-House Diagnostic Laboratory
  • On-Site Veterinary Pharmacy

Rock Hill rocks! With world-renowned sports facilities, rich history and genuine southern hospitality, Rock Hill offers something for everybody. Its proximity to Charlotte means professional sports, a variety of cultural events, and an international airport are easily accessible.  Right in Rock Hill, outdoor activities are plentiful including 250 acres of space at the Rock Hill Outdoor Center, 26 miles of greenway on which to walk, run or bike, and multiple opportunities to boat and paddle on the Catawba River or Lake Wylie. https://www.visitrockhillsc.com/

What can we offer Associate Veterinarian candidates?

  • A flexible schedule to encourage a healthy work/life balance
  • Production bonus options

We offer industry leading benefits that include, full time benefits with a flexible work schedule,  full coverage health insurance, 401K with discretionary employer contribution, Paid Time Off, generous Continuing Education allowance, additional PTO for CE, VIN & Plumbs membership, pet care and partner discounts and reimbursement for membership and license fees.

For more information, or a confidential career discussion, please apply on-line at www.petvetcareers.com or email your resume to Emily Lunsford at elunsford@petvetcarecenters.com.

About Catawba Animal Clinic

At PetVet Care Centers, our hospitals are managed locally, and supported globally, through our connected network. We are a group of over 450 small animal, mixed animal and equine general practice, specialty and emergency hospitals. We appreciate the differences in each community and support the local traditions which have made our member hospitals so successful. Our location based Medical Directors guide quality of care, inform staff development and ensure community connection. Medical decisions, treatment plans and medication choices are made by each of our individual doctors, locally. PetVet supports individualized learning opportunities, and encourages doctors to advance their skill through generous continuing education assistance. We offer a top notch benefits package, which includes additional time off for CE and a variety of benefits to enhance quality of life including creative, flexible scheduling and deferred compensation. PetVet Care Centers wants you to take pride in your workplace, and is committed to investments in equipment and the facility so you can service the community with the highest quality of care.
