Teen Mom Jenelle Evans posts pics of husband David Easons birthday celebration &amp; calls the party

TEEN Mom 2 alum Jenelle Evans posted photos from husband David Eason’s birthday celebration.

The 29-year-old called her husband’s party “a night to remember.”

On Thursday, Jenelle took to Instagram to share four photos that documented them celebrating David’s 33rd birthday.

The first photo in the slide showed the couple happily posing together, while they were then joined by a friend for a selfie.

A third photo caught David kicking one of his legs behind himself as he posed with a mug, followed by a handwritten note that wished him a happy birthday.

Alongside the snaps, the Teen Mom 2 alum wrote: “#HappyBirthday to you, @easondavid88 ! Last night was one to remember. Here’s to 33 years young! ??✨”

Jenelle and David tied the knot in 2017, while they share four-year-old daughter Ensley together.

The MTV star is also the mother to 11-year-old son Jace and six-year-old son Kaiser.

The couple also shared videos on Jenelle’s Instagram Story from their celebration on Wednesday night, which showed them dancing and singing along to the music while the she drank a beer.

Jenelle also took to her stories on Wednesday to share a video of David as he cried over reaching the substantial age.

The TV personality captioned her clip: “Sad he’s 33 today,” as the father of three wept in the background.

Teen Mom 2 star’s post came after her teenage stepdaughter Maryssa worried the public when she shared a message about feeling “bruised and broken.”

The child raised concerns when she posted an emotional picture about feeling “bruised” and “broken” on Instagram, writing: “I stopped venting and started praying cause I don’t need sympathy.

“I need strength- bruised but not broken.”

Teen Mom followers raced to Reddit to discuss the since-deleted cryptic post, which they labeled “sad and “heartbreaking.”

“Jesus. The kid begged to not be sent back to the swamp and she is still forced to live in a swampy hell. I hope she gets out soon and takes all the kids and animals with her,” one expressed.

“This is sad. Poor girl :/” another added while a third user chimed in: “Poor Maryssa. I can’t even imagine what things are like for her.”

David and Jenelle are raising the 13-year-old, who is from one of his previous relationships.
