90210's Shenae Grimes: Jessica Stroup &amp; I Don't Have Body Issues!

90210 star Shenae Grimes is fighting back against criticism that she and co-star Jessica Stroup are too thin.

"I would like to say once again that neither me or Jessica have issues with our weight or bodies," she wrote on her MySpace blog. "I happened to be very small built, and ive always been skinny," she said.

"So I personally think that people need to stop criticizing everyone in the media these days about their issues. "Im sure you wouldnt like it if someone pointed out all of your flaws and all of your mistakes… but thats what the media does to us," she added.

Last week, Us Weekly reported that show producers and costars were considering an intervention of Grimes, 18, who is 5-foot-3 and stands at an estimated 90 pounds, and 21-year-old Stroup, who is 5-foot-8 but weighs 100 to 105 pounds.
