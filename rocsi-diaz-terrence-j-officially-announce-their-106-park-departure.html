Rocsi Diaz, Terrence J Officially Announce Their 106 &amp; Park Departure

Rocsi Diaz and her co-host Terrence J officially announced they are leaving BET’s 106 & Park – after seven long years. Terrence stated,

After an amazing seven years of being on this show, we have decided together that we will be soon leaving 106 and Park as your hosts. This, by no means, has been an easy decision and it has come after a lot of prayer, a lot of thought and a lot of consideration.  First, we’d like to start by thanking BET, this has been an incredible opportunity and we are looking forward to continuing our relationship.  There is no way that we are giving up the keys to the door, not just yet.”
