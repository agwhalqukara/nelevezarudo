Tom Brady Wants to Grow His Hair Long Again

Bringing back the man bun! Tom Brady and his ever-changing hair are ready to grow long again. That means it’s only a matter of time until we see the New England Patriots QB with his hair in the oh-so-trendy man bun style.

In a recent interview with Yahoo! Style, Gisele Bundchen‘s husband, 38, admitted he’s ready for a change. “I think I’m going back to some long hair,” he said. “You know, screw it.”

While Brady, the newest brand ambassador for Tag Heuer, is very intentional about his hair choices, he revealed that he’s much more laid-back in his fashion sense. “I don’t know if anyone considers themselves anything,” he said, responding to a question about whether or not he thinks of himself a style icon. “I do pay attention to fashion. I like clothes and I like watches. I don’t wear suits too often — I’m pretty casual… I pick out my own clothes.”

But what really put a smile on his face was the mention of Bundchen, 35, his wife of six years and mother to Benjamin and Vivian Brady. “She’s my best friend, so we get to do everything together and especially when you’re fortunate to be in a relationship with someone you love to death, you love doing anything with them,” he told the website.
