Becca Tobin, Glee Star, Breaks Silence on Boyfriend Matt Bendik Death

Becca Tobin has broken her silence on the death of her boyfriend Matt Bendik. The Glee actress thanked those who've reached out during this difficult time and shared a picture of the couple via Instagram on Saturday, Aug. 2.

"Thank you all for the love and support," the 28-year-old wrote. "Matt was the most extraordinary man I knew and he will live in my heart forever."

Bendik was found dead at the age of 35 at Philadelphia's Hotel Monaco on July 10. An autopsy was conducted, but the cause of death still remains unclear. Philadelphia police told Us there were "no signs of foul play" at the time.
