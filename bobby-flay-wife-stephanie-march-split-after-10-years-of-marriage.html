Bobby Flay, Wife Stephanie March Split After 10 Years of Marriage

Cooking up a separation. Celebrity chef Bobby Flay and his wife Stephanie March have separated after 10 years of marriage, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

The source close to Flay tells Us that the couple's split happened several weeks ago. Flay has since moved out of their NYC apartment.

This was the third marriage for the Food Network chef, 50, and the first for the Law & Order: SVU actress, 40. Flay was previously married to fellow chef Debra Ponzek from 1991 to 1993, and later wed another TV personality, Kate Connelly, in 1995. He and Connelly share 18-year-old daughter Sophie.

Flay and March married in February 2005 in Manhattan. But this past February, the two did not celebrate their milestone 10th wedding anniversary together. Instead, the source tells Us that Flay was at the Food & Wine Festival down in Florida.

In an interview with CBS Sunday Morning last December, Flay shared March's sentiments about his heavy workload. "I think she’d like me to work a little bit less, for sure," Flay said. "That’s never going to change, you know?"
