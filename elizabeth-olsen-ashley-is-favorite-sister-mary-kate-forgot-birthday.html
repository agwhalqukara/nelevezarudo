Elizabeth Olsen: Ashley Is Favorite Sister, Mary-Kate Forgot Birthday

Put on the spot! During an appearance on Watch What Happens Live on Wednesday, Feb. 19, Elizabeth Olsen was asked by a caller to pick her favorite sister: Mary-Kate or Ashley Olsen? Surprisingly, Elizabeth gave an honest answer about the world famous twin actresses-turned-designers, 27.

“You have two sisters, I also have two sisters, you have to prefer one over the other,” the caller told Elizabeth on the Bravo late night talk show. “Who do you like better: Mary-Kate or Ashley?”

“Now this is a good question,” host Andy Cohen said. “There has to be one that is more in your favor today that you’re more vibing on.”

“Ashley remembered my birthday, but I don’t hold grudges against Mary-Kate forgetting mine,” Elizabeth replied, hesitantly, about the twins. “She was out of town! I should have just said my brother!” the In Secret actress, who celebrated her 25th on Sunday, Feb. 16, added with a laugh.

“Ashley’s in the lead today, but tomorrow MK could do something amazing,” Cohen said.

“We’re all very close!” Elizabeth added. “I’m very close with my brother [actor Trent, 29] as well. I should have just said him. That would have been the easier scapegoat.”

“You are though, to clarify, feeling closer to Ashley today,” Cohen said, teasing his guest.
