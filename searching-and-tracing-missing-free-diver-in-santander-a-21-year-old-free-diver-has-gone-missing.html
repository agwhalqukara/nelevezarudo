Search and Retrieval Operations for Missing Free Diver

Efforts are currently underway to locate a 21-year-old college student and free diver who went missing while spearfishing in Santander town on Christmas Day. The search and retrieval operations, led by the Philippine Coast Guard and the Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office of Santander, are focused on finding Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot. With the assistance of local government forces from Argao and Guihulngan City, the search teams are tirelessly working to locate the missing diver and bring him back safely.

Missing Person Alert Filed

A missing person alert was promptly filed for Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot, the 21-year-old free diver who failed to return after spearfishing at Liloan Dive Point in Liloan, Santander. The alert was raised by his diving buddy, who became concerned when Dayot did not resurface after a five-minute free dive. The authorities were immediately notified, and search and retrieval operations were initiated to locate the missing individual.

Identification of the Missing Person

The missing person has been identified as Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot, a resident of San Sebastian in Samboan town. Dayot, a college student and experienced free diver, ventured out to Liloan Dive Point in Santander on December 25 for a spearfishing expedition with a friend. Unfortunately, he did not return after his dive, leading to his current status as a missing person.

Current Status of the Search

As of the latest update, Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot remains missing. The local government of Samboan released an advisory on social media, urging the public to provide any information that may aid in the search and retrieval efforts. The search teams, comprising of rescue personnel from various agencies, are tirelessly combing the waters and surrounding areas in hopes of locating Dayot and bringing him back to safety.

Discovery of Scuba Gear in Amlan

A significant development in the search and retrieval operations occurred when scuba gear belonging to Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot was found in Amlan town in Negros Oriental, which is located across Santander. This discovery has provided search teams with a crucial lead, indicating the possible direction or location where Dayot may have encountered difficulties. The authorities are now intensifying their efforts in the area, utilizing this new information to narrow down the search and increase the chances of a successful rescue.

Augmentation Forces from Argao and Guihulngan City

In a collaborative effort to enhance the search and retrieval operations, augmentation forces from the local governments of Argao and Guihulngan City have joined the ongoing efforts. These additional personnel bring valuable expertise and resources to the mission, further strengthening the search teams’ capabilities. With their combined efforts, the chances of locating Louis Jullan Jugo Dayot and ensuring his safe return are significantly increased.

Location and Activity

Liloan Dive Point in Santander served as the location for Dayot’s spearfishing activity. Known for its rich marine life and clear waters, it is a popular spot among diving enthusiasts. Dayot, equipped with his diving gear, embarked on his underwater adventure in the early morning hours of December 25.
