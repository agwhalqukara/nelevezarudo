Kim Kardashian &amp; Kanye West Divorce Rumors Resurface

Everyone thought that Kim Kardashian and Kanye West are doing better than ever, regardless of the public issues that Kanye might have been having lately. As you all know, he is involved in an online feud with Drake.

Kanye even said that Drake threatened him and his family and that was the moment when Kim jumped online to defend her hubby.

Kim took to Twitter to praise her husband for being a genius and breaking down boundaries across multiple spheres of influence.

She also made sure to tell Drake that Kanye ‘paved the way for there to be a Drake.’

Now, Hot New Hip Hop reports that the rumors about a potential divorce surfaced again. They wrote that Elle Magazine wrote about an imminent divorce and the rumors turned out to be false.

The magazine was faced with massive criticism for writing this, but since then, more publications are suggesting that these two might break up again.

Some magazines claim that Kim is far from being safe,.especially since Kanye has been tweeting like crazy recently.

Anyway, as a conclusion, fans should reportedly not be worried because some sources close to the family have come forward with similar misinformation many times before, and they turned out to be completely bogus.
