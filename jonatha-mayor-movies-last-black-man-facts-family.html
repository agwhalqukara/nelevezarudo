Jonatha Mayor; Movies, Last Black Man, Facts, Family

Rare are those actors who get a tweet appreciation from a former U.S President and Jonathan Mayors is among one. In late 2019, Barack Obama made a tweet on his 2019 release The Black Man to be one of his most anticipated and liked movies.

American actor Jonathan Mayor is currently known for his role portrayal of David in the mid-2020 American war drama film, Da 5 Bloods. A year before, he did complete justice to step in the role of Montgomery Allen in The Last Black Man.

Jonathan Mayor Bio & Family

The actor par excellence was born on 7 September 1989 in Dallas, Texas. Talking about his family, he has kept their information under wraps away from the pondering media spotlights. The only thing known about his family is his mother is a pastor.

Jonathan went to Duncanville High School. Over there, he won many awards including One-Act Play and TFA Tournaments. Sometime later, he completed his high school graduation in 2008. Then, he chose the University of North Carolina School of the Arts to pursue his Bachelor’s degree. 

Exceptional Movies & Obama’s Tweet on The Last Black Man

The 30-year-old right after his acting graduation commenced his career in the showbiz industry. In 2017, he got the role of Corporal Henry Woodson in the film Hostiles. The next years, he had two exceptional movies like White Boy Rick and Out of Blue.

With his amplifying on the silver screen, he had some pivotal roles in 2019. The year he had four biggest releases including The Last Black Man in San Francisco, Captivate State, Gully, and Jungleland. For his role as Montgomery Allen, he received worldwide acclaim. 

The former U.S President Barrack Obama showed his support towards The Black Man to be one of his favorite TV Shows. Obama made a tweet on 29 December 2019. The Black Man helmed by director Joe Talbot on a budget of $2 million collected $4.6 million at the box office. Jonathan is riding high in his career with one of his brilliant role of David in Da 5 Bloods. The 2020 movie based on the American war is directed by Spike Lee and got a worldwide release on 12 June 2020.

Besides the silver screen, Jonathan has some TV credits including When We Rise (2017) and Lovecraft Country (2020).

Is He Dating Or Married?

Jonathan in public dais is mostly known for his TV and movie roles. Other than that, the 30-year-old refrains to share anything about his personal life. He could be possibly dating or is still to be getting married is still a big mystery to solve.
