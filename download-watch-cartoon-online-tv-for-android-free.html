Download Watch cartoon online tv for Android - Free

The Watch Cartoon Online TV Android APK is a free app to watch cartoons. The app boasts many styles and origins of cartoon types, and the biggest category is the available selection of anime, which are Japanese cartoons.

What is Watch Cartoon Online TV?

It’s an Android APK that allows you to watch anime on your device. There are hundreds of cartoons to choose from. In addition to the most famous anime, there are lots of OVA series. Listings are categorized, including separate lists for English-dubbed and English-subtitled cartoons.

Is Watch Cartoon Online TV free?

Yes. The app is free to download, and watching cartoons is completely free too.

Is Watch Cartoon Online TV safe?

It’s a free Android APK, so it’s not available from the Google Play store, but our research didn't find any red flags concerning this app. However, with any app that you download, you should always read the privacy policy and carefully consider the permissions that it requests. 

How do I download Watch Cartoon Online TV?

There are two simple methods.

From the browser on your Android device

  • Navigate to the APK download site with the downloadable app.
  • Once you tap on the APK file, your device will warn you that files downloaded from ‘unknown’ sources can be harmful. Tap to go ahead.
  • Once it’s downloaded, go to the ‘Downloads’ folder on your device, tap on the ‘APK,’ and follow the prompts to allow it to install.

    • From your desktop

  • Download the APK site to your PC.
  • Ensure that apps from outside the Google Play store are allowed on your Android device.
  • When the APK has downloaded, connect your Android device to your PC as a media device, select the latter.
  • Copy the APK file from your PC to your Android device.
