Eric Benet's Wife Manuela Testolini Pregnant Again, Celebration Photos

Lucia’s going to be a big sister! Eric Benet and his wife Manuela Testolini are expecting their second child, the singer’s rep confirms to Us Weekly exclusively. The couple are already parents to 2-year-old daughter Lucia Bella.

“She is due this summer,” Benet’s rep confirms. The couple announced the happy news to their family and friends by sharing a sweet picture of their toddler making a “to do list.” Some of her tasks included learning how to walk and talk. The only detail not checked off yet? “Become a big sister!”

Benet, 47, and Testolini, 37, tied the knot in July 2011, and welcomed Lucia in December 2012. One month later, the proud parents shared the first photos of their bundle of joy with Us exclusively.
