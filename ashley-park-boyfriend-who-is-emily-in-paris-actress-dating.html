Ashley Park Boyfriend: Who Is Emily in Paris Actress Dating?

Best known for her role as Mindy Chen in the hit comedy series Emily in Paris, Ashley Park is a renowned musical actress with a Tony and a Grammy nomination, each under her name!

While in Emily in Paris, Ashley Park has her eyes set on Paul Forman’s character; she’s not that bold in real life! Preferring to keep things away from prying eyes, many fans wondered whether someone caught Ashley Park’s eyes in real life.

Does Ashley Park Have A Boyfriend?

To all the guys, you may rejoice! Ashley Park is reportedly single and ready to mingle! The actress is notoriously private about her romantic relationships. Still, the lack of her sighted with a mysterious man might indicate that Ashley is now focusing on her career and having fun with friends and family.

She was briefly rumored to be dating her Emily in Paris co-star, Lucas Bravo. The rumors surfaced after they often shared pictures of each other during the series’ first season, and Lucas even visited Ashley in New York!

However, Lucas indirectly debunked the rumors by stating that he has a clear stance against dating co-stars, claiming that a set is “a workplace” and that he “want to keep it professional.”

Benjamin Tyler Cook and Ashley Park

In 2019, Ashley Park was rumored to be dating her Mean Girls co-star, Benjamin Tyler Cook. Fans’ suspicions began to rise after she left a heart emoji in the comment section of one of Benjamin’s Instagram posts.

Furthermore, Ashley and Ben had been attending multiple red-carpet events together that year, including the 73rd Annual Tony Awards. They also spent much time together, which they often shared on Instagram. Unfortunately, their romance didn’t last any longer, as fans noticed the pair no longer follow each other on Instagram.

Oof. The bad blood between ex-lovers, perhaps?

Were Ashley Park and Zach Adkins Dating?

What’s the easiest way to create dating rumors involving you? Yep, bring a plus-one and pose together on the red carpet! At least that was how Ashley Park and Zach Adkins were involved in dating rumors!

The pair attended the American Theatre Wing Annual Gala in 2018. Several months later, Ashley posted a picture of her and Zach on Twitter, wearing matching coats. In the caption, she declared her “proud” of Zach.

Again, their relationship (if there’s any) didn’t last more than several months, as Ashley and Zach also unfollowed each other on social media.

Did Ashley Park Once Date Conrad Ricamora?

In 2016, Ashley Park starred in the Broadway revival of The King and I opposite Conrad Ricamora, portraying each other’s love interest. The pair shared several heated kiss scenes and brilliant chemistry, so that’s only fair if people started to assume something is happening off-camera.

When asked about her first impression of Conrad, Ashley recalled that she thought he had “a sweet smile” and “a great height.” If that’s not enough to fuel fans’ hope of them dating in real life, Conrad also shared that they act like they “are married” because he once got mad at Ashley but managed to get over it and keep hanging out.

Neither Ashley nor Conrad ever addressed the dating rumors, but one thing for sure is that they’re really good friends!
