Naomi Watts News - Us Weekly

Name:Naomi Watts

Hometown:Shoreham, Kent, England

Birthday:September 28, 1968

About

Naomi Watts’ breakthrough role in David Lynch’s Mulholland Drive in 2001 launched her Hollywood career. She went on to star in The Ring (2002), 21 Grams (2003), King Kong (2005), Stay (2005), Mother and Child (2009), The Impossible (2013), Birdman (2014), St. Vincent (2015) and The Glass Castle (2017). Her work in film has earned her one Screen Actors Guild Award from four nominations, along with a BAFTA nod and two Academy Award nominations.

Watts later returned to television with roles on Showtime’s Twin Peaks, Netflix’s Gypsy and The Watcher and Hulu’s Feud: Capote vs. The Swans. The actress welcomed children Sasha and Kai in 2007 and 2008, respectively, with Liev Schreiber, whom she dated from 2005 to 2016. Watts moved on with Gypsy costar Billy Crudup in 2017, and the couple wed in June 2023.
