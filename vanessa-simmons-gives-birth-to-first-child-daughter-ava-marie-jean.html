Vanessa Simmons Gives Birth to First Child, Daughter Ava Marie Jean

Run's a grandpa! Vanessa Simmons and her boyfriend Mike Wayans have welcomed a baby girl, a rep for the star confirms to Us Weekly. The daughter of Run D.M.C.'s Joseph Simmons welcomed her first child on Thursday, Feb. 13 and named her little bundle of joy Ava Marie Jean.

Simmons, 30, first revealed last September that she and actor Wayans, 28, were expecting — and she was already five months along! "We are both very excited about the birth of our first child," the new mom told Us at the time. "We totally were expecting it to be [a] boy all along but got very surprised when we found out it was indeed a girl. I immediately went out and brought a cute [little] outfit for her right after the appointment."
